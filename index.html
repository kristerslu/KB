Self-Picking Adventure

Experience the farm! Here is how our self-picking works in Aizkraukle:

1
Arrive at the farm. Find us easily on your favorite app.
Google Maps Waze
2
Weigh your buckets. Please bring your own bucket to protect nature, or we can provide packaging!
3
Pick the berries. Head out into the fields and pick as much as you like! (Minimum picked amount is 3kg).
4
Weigh the harvest. Return to the station where our employees will weigh your fresh berries.
5
Checkout. We accept cash and cards.
